Pizza

Pizza is a dish from Italy

Pizza is usually round. flat base of leavended wheat-based dough topped with tomatoes, cheese, and often various other ingredients.

Ingredients

  • Bread flour
  • Sugar
  • Salt
  • Yeast
  • Extra virgin olive oil
  • lukewarm water
  • Tomato sauce
  • Pepperoni
  • Cheese

Step-By-Step

  1. Make the dough and roll it out.
  2. Put Tomato sauce on dough.
  3. Add pepperoni and cheese.
  4. Put the pizza in the oven.
