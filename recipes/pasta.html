Pasta Bolognese is a slowly cooked meat-based sauce, and is often used to refer to a tomato-based sauce to which minced meat has been added.

Ingredients

  • Ground beef
  • Tomato sauce
  • Pasta
  • Onion and garlic

Step-By-Step

  1. Boil the pasta: Cook in a large pot of salted water.
  2. Prepare meat sauce: Cook sausage and beef with onion and garlic. Drain well, add the tomato sauce & simmer it for a few minutes to thicken.
